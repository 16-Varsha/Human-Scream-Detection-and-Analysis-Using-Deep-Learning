\# Audio Dataset Sources for Scream Detection



\## Recommended Sources:



\### 1. Freesound.org

\- Search for "scream", "screaming", "yell"

\- Search for "speech", "music", "ambient" for non-scream samples

\- Download samples directly to appropriate folders



\### 2. AudioSet (Google)

\- Contains labeled audio segments

\- Look for "Human voice" and "Screaming" categories



\### 3. ESC-50 Dataset

\- Environmental sound classification dataset

\- Contains various sound categories including human sounds



\### 4. Custom Recording

\- Record your own samples using phone/microphone

\- Get permission from participants

\- Vary conditions: indoor/outdoor, different people, different intensities



\## Collection Strategy:

\- Target: 200-500 samples each (scream/non-scream)

\- Duration: 1-5 seconds per sample

\- Quality: Clear audio, minimal background noise

\- Diversity: Different people, environments, intensities
